About Me

My Story

Hello! I’m Prerna Posa, and if there’s one thing that’s defined my journey so far, it’s curiosity. I’ve always loved figuring out why things work the way they do — from how brands connect with people to how data quietly shapes the decisions we make every day.

I started out studying marketing and international business because I’ve always been fascinated by people, cultures, and the art of communication. But along the way, I found myself drawn to the systems and technology powering it all — how a single dashboard, algorithm, or insight can change the way a business operates. That’s what led me to pursue my master’s in Information Systems at Kelley, where I get to bring together creativity, strategy, and tech to solve real problems.

Outside of school and work, I’m usually exploring something new — whether it’s a new city, a photography project, or an interesting theory I stumbled upon online. I like to think of myself as a mix of analytical and imaginative — someone who finds just as much joy in solving a tough problem as in discovering a new perspective.

My Background

I hold a Bachelor of Science in Business with majors in Marketing and International Business from Indiana University, and I'm currently pursuing my Master of Science in Information Systems at the same institution. My academic journey has provided me with a strong foundation in business strategy, data analytics, and emerging technologies.

My professional experience spans diverse industries and roles, from marketing coordination at Budderfly Inc. to international brand marketing at Suitefood in Milan, Italy. I've also conducted AI risk management research at NRG and performed healthcare marketing analysis at eMedEvents. This variety has taught me to adapt quickly to new industries and requirements while maintaining high standards of analytical rigor and strategic thinking.

My primary goal is to bridge the gap between business strategy and technology implementation, leveraging my unique combination of marketing expertise and information systems knowledge. I'm passionate about:
