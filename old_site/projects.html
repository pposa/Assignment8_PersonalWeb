Patient Transparency Prototype

Patients often do not know how long, how much, or what is next, and hospitals absorb the fallout in calls and rework. I designed a mobile transparency app that unifies live wait times, in-network status, personalized cost estimates with delta chips, EOB-matched billing with one-tap payment/assistance, and clear prior-auth status in one place. Tested with patients, caregivers, and access staff, the prototype boosted confidence to pay without calling and pointed to measurable ops gains (shorter check-ins, fewer billing/auth inquiries).
